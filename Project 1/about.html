About

Studio Ghibli is a renowned Japanese animation studio with a rich history in the animation industry. Founded on June 15, 1985, by visionary directors Hayao Miyazaki and Isao Takahata, the studio has left an incredible mark on the world of animation. Their body of work is a testament to creativity and innovation, captivating audiences worldwide.

Three of the studio's films are among the ten highest-grossing anime feature films made in Japan.
